AI Demand Forecasting Checklist

10-Point Readiness Assessment for Retail AI Implementation

Use this checklist to evaluate whether your retail business is ready to implement AI-powered demand forecasting. Score yourself on each item and calculate your readiness level at the end.

1 Data Infrastructure

  • Historical sales data available
    Minimum 2 years of daily/weekly sales data by SKU
  • Data is centralized and accessible
    Single source of truth via data warehouse or lake
  • Data quality is monitored
    Regular audits, missing values < 5%, consistent formats

2 Business Readiness

  • Executive sponsorship secured
    C-level champion for AI initiative
  • Clear success metrics defined
    Target KPIs: forecast accuracy, inventory turns, stockout reduction
  • Change management plan exists
    Training plan for planners and buyers

3 Technical Capability

  • API integration capability
    Ability to connect AI system to ERP/POS
  • Cloud infrastructure or budget
    GCP, AWS, or Azure environment ready or planned
  • IT resources available
    Team to support implementation and maintenance
  • Security/compliance reviewed
    Data handling policies compatible with AI vendor requirements
